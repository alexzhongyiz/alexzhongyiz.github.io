Alex Zhang

I am a ML and Quant Researcher at a Proprietary trading firm in Chicago. I also worked at Amazon as a Machine Learning Scientist on buyer fraud detection. Prior to that I worked with Prof. Mohammed Abouzaid on my PhD program at Columbia University on the topic of Symplectic Geometry. I went to college at PKU and University of Michigan and was fortunate to do research with Prof. Mel Hochster.

My current research interests lie in the intersection of trading, statistics, and machine learning.

You may also find me on various tennis courts in the suburbs of Chicago.
